Can Ratty Eat it?

A curated list of what can and cannot your pet ratty eat.
Tags General Contribute

Apple

Good and healthy, watch out for seeds.

Apricot

Nutritious, avoid bone.

Bananas

Good except for green.

Brussel sprouts

Only cooked is okay.

Grapes

Healthy; might prevent cancer; high in natural sugars.

Kiwi

Good and healthy in moderation.

Mango

Can causes liver cancer.

Orange

The white part is harmful for male ratties.

Papaya

Good and healthy.

Peach

Good and healthy, beware of stone.

Pear

Good and healthy, watch out for seeds.

Plum

Good and healthy, beware of stone.

Pomegranates

Good and healthy in moderation, messy.

Raw beans

Destroys vitamin a and digestions enzymes in ratty.
This is just a general recommendation based on community experiences. We are not your veterinarian and we don't take responsibility.
