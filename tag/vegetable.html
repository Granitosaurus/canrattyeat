Can Ratty Eat

What can and cannot your ratty eat?
Tags General Contribute

Avocado

Good in small doses, fatty, skin is poisonous.

Beat tops

Toxic, high levels of oxalates.

Broccoli

Healthy in every form, but better cooked.

Caffeine

Unhealthy even in small doses.

Carbonated drinks

Rats can't burp. usually high in sugar

Carrot

Healthy in almost every form.

Celery

Healthy in almost every form.

Corn

Okay when cooked.

Cucumber

Good and healthy.

Lemon

Very bad, kills the ratty

Onion

Can lead to anemia and upsets tummy.

Peas

Good and healthy.

Potato

Boiled or mashed

Red cabbage

Only good when cooked.

Rhubarb

High levels of oxalates which bind up calcium.

Romaine lettuce

Healthy and good to go.

Sugar

Unhealthy, makes ratty fat;

Sweet potato

Only good when cooked.
This is just a general recommendation based on community experiences. We are not your veterinarian and we don't take responsibility.
