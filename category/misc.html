Can Ratty Eat

What can and cannot your ratty eat?
Tags

Apple

Good and healthy, watch out for seeds.

Apricot

Good and healthy.

Avocado

Good in small doses, fatty.

Bananas

Good except for green.

Beat tops

Toxic, high levels of oxalates.

Good, colors the ratty and surroundings.

The blue mold used in cheese is toxic for ratties.

Boysenberries

Good, colors the ratty and surroundings.

Broccoli

Healthy in every form, but better cooked.

Brussel sprouts

Only cooked is okay.

Caffeine

Unhealthy even in small doses.

Carbonated drinks

Rats can't burp. usually high in sugar

Cucumber

Good and healthy.

Grapes

Healthy; might prevent cancer; high in natural sugars.

Green beans

Cooked without spices.

Kiwi

Good and healthy in moderation.

Licorice

Has neurotoxins.

Mango

Can causes liver cancer.

Can lead to anemia and upsets tummy.

Orange

The white part is harmful for male ratties.

Papaya

Good and healthy.

Peach

Good and healthy, beware of stone.

Peanuts

Raw are toxic, fried are okay.

Pear

Good and healthy, watch out for seeds.

Peas

Good and healthy.

Good and healthy, beware of stone.

Pomegranates

Raspberries

Good, colors the ratty and surroundings.

Raw beans

Destroys vitamin a and digestions enzymes in ratty.

Only good when cooked.

Rhubarb

High levels of oxalates which bind up calcium.

Unhealthy, makes ratty fat;

Sweet potato

Only good when cooked.

Tofu

Raw is bad but packaged is okay.
